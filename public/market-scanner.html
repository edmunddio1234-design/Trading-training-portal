How to Use the Market Scanner

The Market Scanner is a powerful stock screener designed to help you identify trading opportunities using technical analysis metrics aligned with the Master Surge Strategy and the S.E.T. Rule.

Key Metrics Explained:

  • RSI (Relative Strength Index): Momentum oscillator that measures the magnitude of price changes. Values below 35 suggest oversold conditions (potential buy setup), above 65 suggest overbought (potential sell signal). Green (30-50) = Normal Strength, Yellow (50-65) = Strong, Red (>65 or <30) = Extreme.
  • Regime: Bull = positive daily change in favorable market conditions, Neutral = consolidation, Bear = negative pressure. Use with Supply/Demand zones from your trading plan.
  • Volume: Simulated daily trading volume. High volume on breaks confirms strength; low volume on moves suggests weakness.
  • Daily Change %: Percentage price movement. Green shows bullish pressure, red shows bearish pressure.

Trading Strategy Integration: Use the scanner to pre-identify stocks in your watchlist that meet your technical criteria, then apply the Master Surge Strategy for precise entry/exit points. Filter by regime to focus on high-probability setups aligned with your market bias.

Pro Tip: Combine screener results with Supply/Demand zone analysis. Stocks in a bullish regime with RSI 30-50 at resistance zones often provide the best risk-reward entries.
